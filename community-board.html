Therapists who specialize in T1D and eating disorders
I wanted to share some resources for finding therapists who understand both diabetes and eating disorders. The We Are Diabetes organization has a directory of specialists. Also, don't be afraid to ask potential therapists directly about their experience with diabetic patients and EDs. The right support makes all the difference.
